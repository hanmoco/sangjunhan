About Me

My name is Sangjun Han, and I am a master's degree student in computer science at Kyushu University. My research interests include natural language processing, machine learning, and data mining. In my free time, I enjoy reading, hiking, and exploring new places.

  • 04/2018 - 03/2023: B.E. from Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Kyushu University
  • 04/2023 - present: Ph.D. from Graduate School of Information Science and Electrical Engineering, Kyushu University

Research

My research focuses on developing machine learning models for natural language processing tasks, such as sentiment analysis and text classification. I am currently working on a project that uses deep learning techniques to improve the accuracy of sentiment analysis in Japanese text.
